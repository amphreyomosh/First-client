We are Dreamsters, a vibrant six-member music group from Kenya, featuring Crevious, DanyG, Mestalon, Holy Savage, Drip City and Baadman. Our mission is to inspire and motivate through our music. We have released around 20 songs, including our debut album, "Nights," which features 18 captivating tracks that highlight our unique sound and lyrical depth.

Our music is available on popular platforms such as YouTube and Spotify, making it accessible for fans worldwide. Each member brings their own distinct style and energy, creating a diverse and rich musical experience that resonates with a wide audience.

Dreamsters is more than just a music group; we are a movement dedicated to turning dreams into reality. Join us on this exciting journey as we continue to produce quality music that inspires action and perseverance. Follow our journey and stay tuned for more releases as we strive to make a lasting impact in the music industry.
